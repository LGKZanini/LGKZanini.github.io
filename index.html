    Hi, my name is Luiz Guilherme, but everyone calls me Luiz. I’m really passionate about applied computing, especially in computer vision, which has been my research focus for the past few years.

    Studies

    I am currently a first-year PhD student in Engineering at École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS), where my research focuses on Multimodal Learning in the process of Emotional Recognition. My advisor is Prof. Alessandro Koerchi.

    Prior to my PhD, I completed my MSc in Eletric Engineering at University of São Paulo (USP), where I focused on Classical Image Processing and Machine Learning for medical images. During my MSc, I was under the supervision of Prof. Fátima de Lourdes dos Santos Nunes.

    I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Computing from Universidade Politécnica de São Paulo, where I also participated in a pre-master’s program while completing my degree in Computer Engineering.
